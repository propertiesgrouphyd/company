2. PURPOSE

The purpose of this Agreement is to define the rights and obligations of the parties with respect to the collection, storage, use, processing and protection of personal data.

3. DEFINITIONS

For the purpose of this Agreement:

  • Personal Data means any information relating to an identifiable individual.
  • Processing means collection, recording, storage, organization, use, disclosure or deletion of data.
  • Data Subject means the individual whose Personal Data is processed.
  • Controller means the entity deciding purposes and means of processing.
  • Processor means the entity processing data on behalf of the Controller.

The Company may process:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Business Information
  • Billing Information
  • GST Details
  • Uploaded Documents
  • User Preferences
  • Device Information

5. PURPOSE OF PROCESSING

Personal Data may be processed for:

  • Providing Services
  • User Authentication

  • Legal Compliance
  • Security Monitoring
  • Improving Products and Services

6. DATA SECURITY

The Company shall implement reasonable technical and organizational measures including:

  • Encryption
  • Secure Authentication
  • Access Controls
  • Regular Backups
  • Audit Logs

Personal Data shall be retained only for:
