1. OBJECTIVE

This Intellectual Property Policy ("Policy") establishes the ownership, protection, use and management of Intellectual Property created, owned or acquired by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED operating under the brand VIDHWAAN .

2. SCOPE

This Policy applies to:

  • Directors
  • Employees
  • Consultants
  • Freelancers
  • Interns
  • Contractors
  • Business Partners

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Intellectual Property includes but is not limited to:

  • Software and Source Code
  • Web Applications
  • Mobile Applications
  • Artificial Intelligence Models
  • Algorithms
  • Databases
  • Business Methods
  • Documents and Templates
  • Logos and Brand Assets
  • Domain Names
  • Designs and Graphics
  • Trade Secrets

4. OWNERSHIP

All Intellectual Property created:

  • By Directors on behalf of the Company,
  • By Employees during employment,
  • By Consultants or Contractors under agreement,
  • Using Company resources,

shall exclusively belong to GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED unless otherwise agreed in writing.

5. BRAND OWNERSHIP

Brand Name VIDHWAAN
Owner GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED
Website www.vidhwaan.com
Status Exclusive Corporate Asset

6. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

The following shall be treated as Confidential Intellectual Property:

  • Source Code
  • Business Plans
  • Software Architecture
  • Databases
  • API Keys
  • Customer Information
  • Financial Information
  • Internal Documents

7. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

No person shall:

  • Copy Company Intellectual Property without authorization.
  • Disclose Confidential Information.
  • Sell or transfer Company IP.
  • Create unauthorized copies.
  • Register trademarks or domains similar to Company assets.

8. OPEN SOURCE SOFTWARE

The Company may use open source software subject to:

  • License Compliance
  • Proper Attribution
  • Legal Review where necessary

Open source components shall not affect ownership of proprietary Company Intellectual Property.

9. TRADEMARKS

The Company may register trademarks, logos and brand assets in India and other countries.

All trademark rights shall belong exclusively to GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED.

10. ENFORCEMENT

Any unauthorized use of Company Intellectual Property may result in:

  • Termination of Contracts
  • Legal Proceedings
  • Recovery of Damages
  • Civil or Criminal Action

11. POLICY ADMINISTRATION

This Policy shall be administered and maintained by NITHIN GIDIGI, Director, who is authorized to protect, manage and enforce Intellectual Property rights of the Company.

12. APPROVAL

This Policy is approved and adopted by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED and shall remain effective until amended or replaced by the Board of Directors.
