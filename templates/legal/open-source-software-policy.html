1. OBJECTIVE

This Open Source Software Policy ("Policy") establishes the rules for acquisition, use, modification, contribution and distribution of Open Source Software by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED operating under the brand VIDHWAAN .

2. PURPOSE

The purpose of this Policy is to:

  • Promote responsible use of Open Source Software.
  • Protect Company Intellectual Property.
  • Ensure license compliance.
  • Reduce legal and security risks.
  • Encourage innovation and collaboration.

3. APPLICABILITY

This Policy applies to:

  • Directors
  • Employees
  • Consultants
  • Freelancers
  • Interns
  • Contract Developers
  • Third Party Development Teams

4. OPEN SOURCE SOFTWARE

Open Source Software includes software distributed under licenses such as:

  • MIT License
  • Apache License 2.0
  • BSD Licenses
  • GNU GPL
  • GNU LGPL
  • Mozilla Public License
  • Any other approved Open Source License

5. APPROVED USE

Open Source Software may be used for:

  • Website Development
  • Business Applications
  • AI Solutions
  • Internal Tools
  • Testing and Development
  • Research and Innovation

6. LICENSE COMPLIANCE

Before using Open Source Software, the following shall be verified:

  • License Terms
  • Distribution Requirements
  • Attribution Requirements
  • Modification Obligations
  • Compatibility with Company Products

No Open Source Software shall be used in violation of its license.

7. SECURITY REQUIREMENTS

All Open Source Software shall be:

  • Downloaded from trusted sources.
  • Reviewed for security vulnerabilities.
  • Updated regularly.
  • Monitored for known security issues.
  • Removed if found unsafe.

8. CONTRIBUTIONS TO OPEN SOURCE

Employees or consultants contributing to Open Source projects on behalf of the Company shall:

  • Obtain necessary approvals.
  • Protect Company Confidential Information.
  • Avoid disclosure of proprietary source code.
  • Comply with project contribution guidelines.

9. COMPANY OWNED SOFTWARE

The Company may release selected software projects as Open Source.

Any such release shall require approval from:

  • Board of Directors
  • Authorized Director
  • Legal & Compliance Team

10. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

The following are prohibited:

  • Using pirated software.
  • Violating Open Source licenses.
  • Uploading Company source code publicly without authorization.
  • Publishing confidential information.
  • Using untrusted software packages.

11. POLICY ADMINISTRATION

This Policy shall be administered and maintained by NITHIN GIDIGI, Director, who is authorized to approve and oversee Open Source Software usage within the Company.

12. APPROVAL

This Policy is approved and adopted by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED and shall remain effective until amended or replaced by the Board of Directors.
