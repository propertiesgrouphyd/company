1. OBJECTIVE

This Artificial Intelligence (AI) Usage Policy ("Policy") establishes the principles governing the development, use, deployment and management of Artificial Intelligence systems by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED operating under the brand VIDHWAAN .

2. PURPOSE

The objectives of this Policy are:

  • Promote responsible and ethical use of AI.
  • Protect privacy and confidential information.
  • Ensure transparency and accountability.
  • Maintain legal and regulatory compliance.
  • Encourage innovation while minimizing risks.

3. APPLICABILITY

This Policy applies to:

  • Directors
  • Employees
  • Consultants
  • Interns
  • Freelancers
  • Contractors
  • Third Party Service Providers

4. APPROVED USES OF AI

AI technologies may be used for:

  • Business Applications
  • Customer Support
  • Document Generation
  • Software Development Assistance
  • Data Analysis
  • Research & Innovation
  • Automation of Business Processes
  • AI Powered Products and Services

5. PROHIBITED USES

AI shall not be used for:

  • Illegal activities.
  • Unauthorized surveillance.
  • Creating misleading or fraudulent content.
  • Violating intellectual property rights.
  • Processing confidential information without authorization.
  • Discrimination or unfair treatment.

6. DATA PRIVACY

Users of AI systems shall:

  • Protect personal information.
  • Avoid sharing confidential data with unauthorized AI systems.
  • Comply with Privacy Policies.
  • Follow Data Processing Agreements.

Sensitive Company information shall not be uploaded to external AI systems without approval.

7. HUMAN OVERSIGHT

AI generated outputs shall be reviewed by authorized personnel before being:

  • Published.
  • Sent to customers.
  • Filed with Government Authorities.
  • Used in legal or financial matters.

8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All AI systems, prompts, workflows, documents, software and outputs developed by or for the Company shall belong exclusively to GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED unless otherwise agreed in writing.

9. THIRD PARTY AI SERVICES

The Company may use third party AI services subject to:

  • Security Review.
  • Privacy Compliance.
  • License Compliance.
  • Business Approval.

10. SECURITY

AI systems shall be protected through:

  • Secure Authentication.
  • Access Controls.
  • Encrypted Storage.
  • Audit Logs.
  • Periodic Security Reviews.

11. POLICY ADMINISTRATION

This Policy shall be administered and maintained by NITHIN GIDIGI, Director, who is authorized to oversee the responsible use of Artificial Intelligence within the Company.

12. APPROVAL

This Policy is approved and adopted by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED and shall remain effective until amended or replaced by the Board of Directors.
