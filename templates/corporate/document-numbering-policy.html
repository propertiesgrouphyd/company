1. OBJECTIVE

This Policy establishes a uniform system for numbering, versioning, classifying, storing and preserving all corporate documents of GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED .

The objective of this policy is to ensure consistency, traceability, audit readiness and long-term preservation of all corporate records.

3. NUMBERING FORMAT

Every document shall follow the format:

PREFIX - YEAR - SERIAL NUMBER

Example:

  • BM-2026-001
  • BNK-2026-003
  • ROC-AOC4-2027-001
  • CORP-2026-007

4. VERSION CONTROL

Version Meaning
0.1 Draft
0.5 Under Review
1.0 Approved
1.1 Minor Revision
2.0 Major Revision

5. DOCUMENT STATUS

Status Meaning
Draft Under Preparation
Review Under Verification
Approved Official Document
Archived Historical Record
Cancelled No Longer Valid

6. FILE NAMING CONVENTION

All digital documents shall be stored using the following naming format:

DOCUMENTNO_DOCUMENTNAME_VERSION.pdf

Examples:

  • BM-2026-001_BOARD_NOTICE_V1.pdf
  • BNK-2026-001_BANK_OPENING_V1.pdf
  • ROC-MGT7-2027-001_MGT7_RECORD_V1.pdf
  • CORP-2026-001_COMPANY_PROFILE_V1.pdf

7. DIGITAL SIGNATURES

All important documents may be digitally signed using valid Digital Signature Certificates (DSC).

The digitally signed PDF shall be treated as the official record of the Company.

8. RECORD PRESERVATION

Record Type Retention
Board Records Permanent

9. POLICY ADMINISTRATION

This Policy shall be administered and maintained by NITHIN GIDIGI, Director, who is authorized by the Board to maintain corporate records and digital archives of the Company.

10. APPROVAL

This Policy is approved and adopted by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED and shall remain in force until amended or replaced by the Board of Directors.
