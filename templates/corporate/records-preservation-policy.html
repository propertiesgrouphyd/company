RECORDS PRESERVATION POLICY
Document No : CORP-POL-2026-003
Version : 1.0
Status : Approved
Effective Date : 21 June 2026

1. OBJECTIVE

This Policy establishes the principles and procedures for preservation, storage, protection and disposal of records maintained by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED .

The Company is committed to maintaining accurate, secure and accessible records throughout its existence.

2. APPLICABILITY

This Policy applies to:

  • Physical Records
  • Electronic Records
  • Digital Signatures
  • PDF Documents
  • Statutory Registers
  • Financial Records
  • Tax Records
  • Corporate Policies
  • Board Records

3. RECORD CATEGORIES

Category Retention Period
Certificate of Incorporation Permanent
MOA & AOA Permanent

Register of Directors Permanent
ROC Filings Permanent

Bank Records Permanent
Financial Statements Permanent
Corporate Policies Permanent

4. ELECTRONIC RECORDS

The Company may maintain records in electronic form including:

  • HTML Documents
  • PDF Documents
  • JSON Records
  • Scanned Copies
  • Digitally Signed Files
  • Corporate Databases

Electronic records shall be considered official Company records.

5. DIGITAL SIGNATURE RECORDS

Digitally signed documents shall be stored together with:

  • Original Source File
  • Signed PDF
  • Supporting Attachments
  • Revision History
  • Government Acknowledgements

6. STORAGE STRUCTURE

Records shall be stored in the following structure: 


corporate/

├── board/

├── directors/

├── shares/

├── auditor/

├── bank/

├── roc/

├── tax/

├── hr/

├── legal/

├── policies/

└── archive/

7. BACKUP POLICY

The Company shall maintain:

  • Primary Digital Storage
  • Secondary Backup Storage
  • Offline Backup Copies
  • Annual Archive Backups

8. ARCHIVE POLICY

Archived records shall:

  • Retain Original Document Numbers
  • Retain Digital Signatures
  • Retain Revision History
  • Remain Read-Only
  • Be available for audit and verification

9. RESPONSIBILITY

The Board authorizes NITHIN GIDIGI, Director, to maintain, preserve, organize and safeguard all corporate records of the Company.

10. APPROVAL

This Policy is approved and adopted by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED and shall remain in force until modified or replaced by the Board of Directors.
