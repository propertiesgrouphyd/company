DIGITAL SIGNATURE POLICY

Version : 1.0

1. OBJECTIVE

This Policy establishes the rules and procedures for use of Digital Signatures, electronic records and digitally signed documents by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED in accordance with applicable laws of India.

2. PURPOSE

The Company adopts Digital Signature Certificates (DSC) to:

  • Authenticate electronic records.
  • Sign MCA filings.
  • Sign ROC forms.
  • Sign Board Records.
  • Sign Corporate Documents.
  • Maintain paperless corporate records.

3. AUTHORIZED DSC HOLDERS

Name Designation Authority
NITHIN GIDIGI Director Authorized to digitally sign Company documents and Government filings.
GIDIGI ANKAIAH Director Authorized to digitally sign documents requiring Board approval.

4. DOCUMENTS ELIGIBLE FOR DSC

  • Board Meeting Notices
  • Minutes of Meetings
  • Board Resolutions
  • Share Certificates
  • ROC Compliance Records
  • MCA eForms
  • Tax Records
  • Banking Documents
  • Corporate Policies
  • Agreements and Contracts

5. ELECTRONIC RECORDS

The Company may create, preserve and maintain all corporate records in electronic format.

Electronic documents together with Digital Signatures and PDF archives shall constitute official Company records.

6. PDF DOCUMENT STANDARD

All official PDF documents shall:

  • Be generated in A4 format.
  • Contain official Letterhead.
  • Carry Document Number.
  • Carry Version Number.
  • Carry Status.
  • Be stored permanently.

7. QR CODE VERIFICATION

The Company may include QR Codes in official documents for:

  • Document verification.
  • Website verification.
  • Corporate record references.

8. SECURITY

Digital Signature tokens, passwords, certificates and private keys shall remain under the control of the authorized holder.

Unauthorized use of Digital Signatures is strictly prohibited.

9. RECORD PRESERVATION

All digitally signed records shall be preserved permanently along with:

  • Original HTML Documents
  • Signed PDFs
  • Revision History
  • Supporting Attachments
  • Government Acknowledgements

10. POLICY ADMINISTRATION

This Policy shall be administered and maintained by NITHIN GIDIGI, Director, who is authorized by the Board to manage Corporate Records, Electronic Records and Digital Archives.

11. APPROVAL

This Policy is approved and adopted by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED and shall remain in force until modified or replaced by the Board of Directors.
