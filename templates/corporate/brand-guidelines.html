5. LETTERHEAD STANDARD

Every official document shall contain:

  • VIDHWAAN Logo
  • VIDHWAAN Brand Name
  • GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED
  • CIN
  • PAN
  • GSTIN (when available)
  • Website

6. LOGO USAGE

The VIDHWAAN Logo shall:

  • Always maintain original proportions.
  • Not be stretched or distorted.
  • Appear clearly on light or dark backgrounds.
  • Be used in official documents only.

7. DOCUMENT DESIGN

All official documents shall follow:

  • A4 Size
  • White Background
  • Black Typography
  • Gold Accent Line
  • Subtle Watermark
  • Official Footer
  • Document Number
  • Version Number
  • DSC Support

8. WEBSITE BRANDING

The official website of the Company is:

www.vidhwaan.com

The website shall maintain:

  • Corporate Black Theme
  • Gold Highlights
  • Minimal Design
  • Fast Loading
  • Mobile Friendly Interface
  • Secure Digital Experience

9. CORPORATE SEAL

The Company may maintain an official Digital Corporate Seal for use in:

  • Share Certificates
  • Board Records
  • Corporate Policies
  • Certificates
  • Special Documents

10. APPROVAL

These Brand Guidelines are approved and adopted by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED and shall remain effective until amended by the Board of Directors.
