1. OBJECTIVE

This Policy establishes the official Letterhead design and layout standards of GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED for all corporate documents, records and communications.

3. HEADER STRUCTURE

The official letterhead header shall contain:

  • VIDHWAAN Logo positioned on the left.
  • VIDHWAAN brand name centered.
  • GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED below the brand name.
  • Corporate details placed below the Company Name.
  • No side panel containing CIN, PAN or GST.

4. CORPORATE DETAILS FORMAT

Corporate details shall appear centered below the Company Name in a single line.

Example:

CIN : U62099AP2026PTC126392   |   PAN : AANCG1335J   |   GSTIN : To be Updated   |   www.vidhwaan.com

5. WATERMARK

Official documents may contain a watermark.

The watermark shall:

  • Use VIDHWAAN Logo only.
  • Remain centered.
  • Opacity below 5%.
  • Never affect readability.

6. OFFICIAL COLORS

Color HEX Code
Corporate Black #111111
Royal Gold #C9A227
Soft Gold #E7D38A
White #FFFFFF

7. DOCUMENT SIZE

All official documents shall use:

  • A4 Portrait
  • 210 mm Width
  • 297 mm Height
  • Minimum 15 mm margins
  • PDF safe layout

8. FOOTER

The footer shall contain:

  • Registered Office Address.
  • VIDHWAAN Brand Name.
  • Website Address.
  • Page Number.

9. DIGITAL SIGNATURE AREA

Every document may contain Digital Signature sections.

Digital Signatures shall:

  • Appear only at the end of the document.
  • Support DSC signing.
  • Support PDF signing.
  • Remain clear and printable.

10. CORPORATE SEAL

The official Digital Corporate Seal may appear on:

  • Share Certificates
  • Board Records
  • Corporate Certificates
  • Special Corporate Documents

11. APPROVAL

This Letterhead Policy is approved and adopted by GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED and shall remain effective until amended or replaced by the Board of Directors.
