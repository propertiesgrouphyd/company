The First Meeting of the Board of Directors of GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED was held on 21 June 2026 at 11:00 AM at the Registered Office of the Company situated at House of Gidigi Ankaiah, Utukur Gram Panchayat, Utukur, Sydapuram, Nellore - 524407, Andhra Pradesh.

DIRECTORS PRESENT

  • NITHIN GIDIGI - Director
  • GIDIGI ANKAIAH - Director

ITEM NO. 1

TAKING NOTE OF CERTIFICATE OF INCORPORATION

The Board took note of the Certificate of Incorporation issued by the Registrar of Companies and recorded that the Company has been incorporated as GIDIGI TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED.

ITEM NO. 2

TAKING NOTE OF MEMORANDUM AND ARTICLES OF ASSOCIATION

The Board took note of the Memorandum of Association and Articles of Association of the Company and resolved to preserve the same electronically and physically as part of permanent corporate records.

ITEM NO. 3

AUTHORIZATION OF CORPORATE ADMINISTRATION

The Board discussed the requirement of effective management and administration of the affairs of the Company.

After discussion, the Board unanimously resolved that Mr. NITHIN GIDIGI, Director, be and is hereby authorized to manage and undertake the following on behalf of the Company:

  • Corporate Administration
  • Government Filings
  • Banking Operations
  • Preparation and Maintenance of Corporate Documents
  • Maintenance of Books and Statutory Registers
  • Representing the Company before Government Authorities
  • Execution of Agreements and Routine Business Documents as authorized by the Board

The Board further resolved that all acts done by him within the scope of the above authority shall be treated as acts done on behalf of the Company.

ITEM NO. 4

OPENING OF BANK ACCOUNT

The Board discussed opening of Bank Account(s) in the name of the Company.

After discussion, Mr. NITHIN GIDIGI is hereby authorized to:

  • Select Bank and Branch
  • Complete KYC
  • Submit Company Documents
  • Sign Applications
  • Obtain Internet Banking
  • Operate the Account as per Board authorization

ITEM NO. 5

APPOINTMENT OF FIRST STATUTORY AUDITOR

The Board discussed appointment of the First Statutory Auditor of the Company.

The Board noted receipt of:

  • Consent to act as Auditor
  • Certificate under Section 141 of the Companies Act, 2013
  • Firm Registration Number (FRN)

ITEM NO. 6

MAINTENANCE OF ELECTRONIC RECORDS

CONCLUSION
